About Founder

FirstName is a passionate leadership, power skills and lean-agile project/program management trainer and consultant. He has over 20 years of experience in developing, leading, driving and managing complex software projects in various sectors such as Stock Market, Online Games (Websites and Mobiles), US Healthcare, Wireless, Floral and Learning & Development.

He is a PMI Certified PMP and PMI-ATP-Instructor-PMP. He holds internationally recognized Lean Six Sigma Black Belt & Lean Six Sigma Green Belt Certificates and was SAFe Agilist. He is a certified Master Trainer (Masters Diploma in Training and Development) from Indian Academy of Training and Development (IATD), Chennai, India. He has conducted 13 workshops on PMP, 2 on Leadership and 1 on Business Communication.
